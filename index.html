Security First

Your Data, Protected at Every Layer

NALAM AI is built with a zero-trust security architecture ensuring your medical records are never exposed without your explicit consent.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

All health records are encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you and authorized parties can decrypt your data.

🪪

Unique ID Authentication

Every user receives a unique NALAM ID. Access to any record requires verified identity matching.

🛡️

Authorized Access Only

Patients explicitly grant access to healthcare professionals. Unauthorized access attempts are blocked and logged.

📋

Full Audit Trail

Every record view, edit, and share is logged with timestamps, giving you complete visibility over your data.
